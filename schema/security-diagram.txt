* Security Models
** Bell-LaPadula Model
*** Focus: Confidentiality
*** Rules:
**** No read-up (Simple Security Property)
**** No write-down (*-Property)
*** Use Case Example: Military/Government systems

** Biba Integrity Model
*** Focus: Integrity
*** Rules:
**** No read-down (Simple Integrity Property)
**** No write-up (Integrity *-Property)
*** Use Case Example: Financial/Healthcare systems

** Clark-Wilson Model
*** Focus: Integrity + Business Workflow
*** Principles:
**** Well-formed transactions
**** Separation of duties
**** Access through transformation procedures
**** Audit logging
*** Use Case Example: Business/Commercial systems

* NFT Standards on Ethereum
** ERC-721
*** Unique tokens with ownership
*** Basis for most NFTs
*** Supports metadata and transfer
** ERC-1155
*** Multi-token standard
*** Supports both fungible and non-fungible tokens
*** Batch transfers
** Semi-Fungible Tokens
*** ERC-3525: Supports partial ownership and fungibility
** Rentable NFTs
*** ERC-4907: Time-based ownership with rental roles
** Soulbound NFTs
*** ERC-5114: Soulbound Badge - Non-transferable identity tokens
*** ERC-5192: Minimal Soulbound NFTs - No transfer functionality
*** ERC-5484: Consensual Soulbound Tokens - Explicit binding agreement
** Composable NFTs
*** ERC-998: Parent-child NFT relationships
*** ERC-5773: Context-Dependent Multi-Asset Token
*** ERC-6059: Parent-Governed Nestable NFTs
*** ERC-6220: Equippable Parts for Composable NFTs
** Extendable Metadata
*** ERC-3664: Extensible metadata system for NFTs
** Royalty Payment
*** ERC-2771: Standardized royalty payments for creators
** Multi-Privilege Management
*** ERC-5496: Additional privileges and roles in NFTs
** Custom Errors for NFTs
*** ERC-6093: Common error handling for token transactions
** Token-Bound Accounts
*** ERC-6551: Smart contract wallets for NFTs
** ERC-20/ERC-721 Pair
*** ERC-7631: DN-404 - Dual Nature Token Pair (Fungible & Non-Fungible)
*** ERC-7651: Fractionally Represented Non-Fungible Token
